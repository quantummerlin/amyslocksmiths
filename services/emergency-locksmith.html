Emergency Locksmith FAQs

We aim to arrive as quickly as possible. In many cases, we could be with you within 20–30 minutes, depending on your location, time of day, and current demand. Calling directly always gives you the fastest response.

After-hours and emergency callouts may attract a higher rate due to the time of day. We will always give you a clear price estimate before heading out — no hidden costs.

We always try the least invasive method first. In most cases, a skilled locksmith can open a lock without causing any damage. If the lock must be drilled (e.g., after a break-in), we'll explain why and advise on replacement options.

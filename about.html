The Story

How It All Started

Amy and Simon grew up in Melbourne's western suburbs and have always had a deep connection to the Brimbank community. After training as a locksmith and building experience in the trade, Simon founded the business with Amy joining shortly after.

From the very beginning, their focus was simple: provide the kind of service they'd want for their own family. That means answering the phone at 2am when someone is locked out. That means explaining what the problem is in plain language. That means giving a fair price without gouging someone who's already stressed.

That approach has earned them a loyal following of customers who return time and again — and refer their friends, family, and neighbours without hesitation.

Today, Amy's Locksmiths handles everything from residential emergency lockouts and car unlockings to commercial security upgrades and master key systems. But the values have never changed.
